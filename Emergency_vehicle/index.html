How the AI Works

This system uses Artificial Intelligence concepts such as search algorithms, intelligent agents, and rule-based decision-making to optimize traffic and prioritize emergency vehicles. When an ambulance is detected, the AI engine uses Breadth-First Search (BFS) to find the shortest path to its destination. It proactively overrides upcoming traffic signals along this path turning them GREEN, while halting cross-traffic to ensure a clear, collision-free priority corridor.
